About Us


TravelEasy.com started in 2010 and it was founded by Jason Collins, Linda Essien and Michael Igbonoba. The sole aim of creating this travelling agency was to make travelling easy for Nigerians and solve further problems related to travelling. Currently, We have offices in major cities in Nigeria namely Port-Harcourt, Abuja, Calabar, Enugu and Lagos which is currently our headquarters.

Our mission


It is popularly said that "Travel is good for the soul" and it is on this saying that TravelEasy.com has made it mission to make travelling preparations for clients a stress free process by helping them planning their trips for vacations. With our top notch services we are determined to make our clients happy and travelling a wonderful experience.
